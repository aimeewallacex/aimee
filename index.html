Some random facts about cats... its believed that cats are the only mammals that dont taste sweetness.

cats can sleep for an average of 13-14 hours a day. some cats can jump up to six times their height.

cats developed meowing to get a humans attention.

body language in cats... you can tell a lot about a cats mood by their tail. cats have sevral signs that can show overstimulation.

if a cats tail is upright and slightly curved at the top and/or walking towards you, this is a friendly greeting.

a cats slow blink is a sign of trust and love. a cat having their tummy exposed is another sign of trust.

if a cat stares without blinking, this could be them feeling curious or suprised- but also staring at your cat to them can seem like aggression!
